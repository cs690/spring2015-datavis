    How to use this visualization:

    • Move the pointer over an element: Show data on a tooltip.
    • Click X/Y labels: Show brief explanation about the label on a notification box.
    • Click a "Sort" check box: Sort charts in the same column.
    • Click a dot in the scatterplot: Keep the tooltip open.
